FAQ

Common
Questions

Does HydroSmart need a subscription? +
No. HydroSmart is a one-time purchase with full app access included — forever. We'll never paywall your own data behind a monthly fee.
Is the sensor accurate for all liquids? +
The sensor is calibrated for water and works with most beverages. Very thick liquids like smoothies may affect accuracy slightly. For hydration tracking, it's optimised for water intake.
How do I clean it? Is it dishwasher safe? +
The body is dishwasher safe (top rack). We recommend hand washing the lid to preserve the electronics. The sensor module is sealed and waterproof.
What's the return policy? +
30-day no-questions-asked return policy. If you don't love it, ship it back for a full refund. We cover return shipping for customers in the US and EU.
How long does shipping take? +
US standard shipping is 3–5 business days. Express is 1–2 days. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days depending on location.
Will it work with my smartwatch? +
Yes. HydroSmart syncs with Apple Watch (via Apple Health), Garmin, Fitbit and Wear OS devices. Pro users get direct Garmin Connect integration.
